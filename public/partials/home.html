What is Xchem?

Xchem is a database of nearly 70,000† brands that manufacture cosmetics and other topical products (including makeup, sunblock, deoderant, baby products, lotions and nail polish just to name a few.) All of these brands have chemical ingredients, which we know — but many contain ingredients that are toxic. Search for your brands to find chemical ingredients, reference our list of toxic ingredients to see what health concerns you should know about.
